Absolut Cocktail Recipes

Discover four iconic Absolut cocktails, each crafted for a unique and unforgettable experience.

Lemon Drop

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Absolut Citron
  • 3/4 oz Triple Sec
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Sugar (for rim)

Steps

  1. Rim a martini glass with sugar.
  2. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  3. Strain into the glass and garnish with a lemon twist.

Mango Tango

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Absolut Mango
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Pineapple wedge (garnish)

Steps

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  2. Strain into a chilled glass.
  3. Garnish with a pineapple wedge.

Raspberri Cosmo

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Absolut Raspberri
  • 1 oz cranberry juice
  • 1/2 oz triple sec
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Fresh raspberries (garnish)

Steps

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  2. Strain into a martini glass.
  3. Garnish with fresh raspberries.

Vanilla Pear

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Absolut Vanilla
  • 1 oz pear nectar
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Pear slice (garnish)

Steps

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  2. Strain into a cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a pear slice.
